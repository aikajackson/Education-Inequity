The Issue

  • Students' social status often determines education quality

  • Governments distributing resources based on political pressures, not needs

The Impact

  • Unfitting curriculum = detached students
  • High school dropouts less than 33% likely to find employment
  • Low income areas lack sufficient instructional resources
  • Blocks off higher education and leads to financial struggles

What you can do

  • Take initiative by contacting a state representative to advocate
  • Organize fundraisers to raise resources for students in your community

What school districts can do

  • Appeal to government for policy changes (increased funding for schools in need)
  • Diversify teaching methods; offer online courses
  • Improve teacher collaboration, organization, and skill
